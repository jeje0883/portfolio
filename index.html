About Me

Hello! I'm Jay, a former chemical engineer who discovered a deep passion for software development. My journey began in the labs and plants, where I constantly sought efficient solutions to complex chemical processes. Facing repetitive tasks and data analysis challenges, I started exploring programming as a tool to automate and optimize my work.

This exploration ignited a fascination with coding, leading me to delve deeper into the world of software engineering. I realized that my true passion lay in creating innovative software solutions that can make a significant impact. Transitioning into software development has allowed me to blend my problem-solving skills with creativity, and I'm excited to build applications that improve lives and drive progress.

Now, as a full-stack web developer, I bring a unique perspective from my engineering background to every project I undertake. I'm dedicated to continuous learning and eager to contribute to meaningful projects that challenge me to grow.
